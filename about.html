About

Tim Bynum Productions is an independent studio producing original film, television, and live experiences rooted in Southern authenticity and universal heart. Founded by creator-producer Tim Bynum, our work champions raw stories, sharp humor, and red-carpet polish—built right here in Memphis.

What we do

  • Original series & features: concept → script → cast → production → delivery
  • Distribution strategy: festival runs, theatrical one-nighters, and streaming
  • Event production: private premieres, talkbacks, and brand partnerships
  • Community impact: youth arts collaborations and local talent pipelines

Capabilities

Creative development, budgeting, scheduling, casting, location management, line producing, post supervision, marketing, PR kits, and sponsor integrations.

Stories that move. Moments that matter.

Based in Memphis, Tennessee.
